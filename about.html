About Us

OYIWONA MAMA YOUTH EDUCATION AND EMPOWERMENT FOUNDATION (OMYEEF) is a non-profit, non-governmental organization committed to improving the lives of young people through quality education, skills development, leadership training, entrepreneurship, and community empowerment. We believe that every young person deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential and become a positive force in society.

Our Vision

To build a society where every young person has access to quality education, equal opportunities, and the skills needed to become self-reliant, responsible, and impactful leaders.

Our Mission

To empower young people by promoting access to education, vocational and entrepreneurial skills, mentorship, leadership development, and community-based initiatives that reduce poverty, promote social inclusion, and inspire sustainable development.

Our Objectives
(What We Want to Achieve)

  • Promote quality education and lifelong learning opportunities for young people.
  • Empower youths with vocational, digital, and entrepreneurial skills for sustainable livelihoods.
  • Support disadvantaged and vulnerable youths through scholarships, mentorship, and educational assistance.
  • Foster leadership, innovation, and civic responsibility among young people.
  • Promote gender equality, inclusion, peacebuilding, and social justice.
  • Partner with governments, development organizations, educational institutions, and the private sector to create opportunities for youth development.
  • Encourage community development initiatives that improve health, education, environmental sustainability, and economic growth.
  • Build a generation of confident, skilled, and productive young leaders who contribute positively to national and global development.
